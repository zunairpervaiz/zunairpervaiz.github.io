About me

With 6 years of experience in mobile development, I specialize in building scalable, user-centric apps using Flutter, Kotlin, Java, and Swift. Currently working as a Team Lead and Project Manager, I lead cross-functional teams, plan projects end-to-end, and ensure on-time, high-quality delivery. I've successfully delivered multiple mobile projects from concept to deployment, combining hands-on development with strong stakeholder communication. I focus on clean architecture, performance optimization, and intuitive UX.

As a leader, I mentor developers, streamline workflows, and bridge the gap between technical execution and business strategy. My project management approach keeps teams aligned and projects on track.

I also run a YouTube channel where I share Flutter tutorials, dev tips, and real-world project insights to help other developers grow. Let’s connect and build something impactful together.
